Tailored Services for You

We offer various services and solutions to university health centres and their users (students, staff, and communities), such as:

Appointment Scheduling

Students can easily book, cancel, or reschedule their appointments with the health centre online or via mobile app, and receive reminders and notifications.

Pharmacy Services

Students can order, pay, and receive their prescriptions and medications from the health centre or nearby pharmacies, and get discounts and coupons.

Supply

Health centres can connect with reliable and affordable suppliers of medical equipment, drugs, and supplies, and manage their inventory and procurement processes.

Telemedicine

Students can access remote consultation and diagnosis services from the health centre or other hospitals and clinics, and get referrals and second opinions.

Emergency Care

Access 24/7 urgent care services for immediate health needs.
In case of emergencies, order an ambulance promptly.

Health Education

Students can access health education and promotion materials and programs that help them prevent and manage common health issues, and get personalized health tips and reminders.

Transforming Healthcare

Welcome to CampusCare, where we are dedicated to providing progressive and affordable healthcare solutions tailored for students. Our commitment goes beyond routine work; we take pride in delivering innovative and accessible healthcare options for everyone.

At CampusCare, we understand the unique healthcare needs of students navigating the challenges of academic life. Our services, accessible on mobile and online platforms, aim to simplify healthcare, making it an integral part of the student experience.

By using CampusCare, university health centres can benefit from: Improved quality and timeliness of health care delivery, and increased satisfaction and trust of the users.
Reduced cost and waste of health care resources, and increased efficiency and effectiveness of health care operations.
Enhanced collaboration and communication with other health care providers and stakeholders, and increased value and impact of health care programs.
Increased awareness and utilization of health care services, and improved health outcomes and well-being of the users.
We are looking for university health centres that are interested in partnering with us, and becoming early adopters and testers of our platform.
As a partner, you will get:
Free access and trial of our platform and services, and priority support and feedback.
Exclusive discounts and incentives for your users and staff, and revenue sharing opportunities.
Recognition and exposure as an innovative and progressive health care provider, and access to a network of health tech experts and enthusiasts.

Join us in our mission to ensure that students receive the quality, judgment-free healthcare they deserve. We take pride in being more than just a healthcare provider – we are a partner in the well-being of every student we serve.
